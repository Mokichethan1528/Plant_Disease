Preventive steps for disease control in Brinjal Plant:

1.Taro Leaf Blight (caused by Phytophthora colocasiae):

  • Use copper-based fungicides as a preventative measure, says the TNAU Agritech Portal.
  • Hot water treatment of corms before planting can help to control Phytophthora.
  • Isolate plantings to prevent disease spread.

    • 2.Soft Rot (various fungi):

  • Ensure good drainage to prevent waterlogged conditions.
  • Use bio-control agents like Trichoderma spp. to suppress the pathogen.
  • Avoid wounding the corms during harvest and handling.

    • 3.Pythium Root and Corm Rot:

  • Maintain proper soil pH (6.0-6.8) by adding lime if necessary, according to the TNAU Agritech Portal.
  • Monitor calcium levels in the leaves and maintain adequate calcium concentrations.
